Weekly Journal

Week 2 Entry

1. How did you approach mobile-first design?
I began by designing for small screens first, using simple layouts and stacking content vertically. Then I used media queries to rearrange elements on larger screens.

2. What was the most useful HTML/CSS concept this week?
Flexbox was extremely helpful for creating responsive navigation and flexible page layouts.

3. What was most challenging?
Understanding how media queries apply at different breakpoints was initially confusing, but practice made it easier.
